# 📜 Discord Community Rules

### Welcome to our Melody Family community!
Different families, one community — let’s keep this server fun, respectful, and welcoming for everyone.

</div>

---

## 1. Respect Everyone
Friendly banter is okay, personal attacks and toxic behavior are not.

## 2. Different Families, Same Community
Rivalries stay inside the game. Outside the game, we treat each other like teammates.

## 3. No Drama & Unnecessary Fights
Solve issues calmly or ask moderators for help.

## 4. Keep Chats Clean & Comfortable
Avoid spam, offensive content, and anything that ruins the vibe.

## 5. Help New and Returning Players
A small guide or kind answer can make someone stay in the community.

## 6. Use Channels Properly
Keep topics in the correct channels to avoid clutter.

## 7. Have Fun & Make Memories
Events, grinding, raids, or random talks — enjoy the community together.
